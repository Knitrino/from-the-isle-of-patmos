## The view from here

Last night, we communed with the stars and each other. We shared a bottle of wine on the veranda, laughing over stories and made up myths. In Greece, surely the bright planet Venus that hovers near the moon goes by the name Planiti Aphroditi! Steven saw 6 shooting stars and Alison saw 1 (and 1 UFO that shot horizontally across the sky exploding in a flash of red). Andrea and Barbara saw none, perhaps because they look deeply inward. We're not sure the evening can be topped, but we certainly will try.
