## Living the mermaid life
Swimming in the salty sea is otherworldly. The cold shock of the water on sweaty skin as we submerge our bodies, the release from gravity like floating in the space station, we bob effortlessly in the buoyant sea.

One fish, 200 hundred fish, red fish, bright green fish! Tiny fish students in their schools, sea grass waving in the water breeze. We breathed underwater like mermaids, exploring a world that most mammals never see.
