## An afternoon excursion

Today, we journeyed into Skala in our tiny Fiat (with an extended back seat for the kids!) A visit to the flip floppery brought some success, and an outstanding lunch with local offerings filled us to sleepiness. Our ambitious itinerary included the procurement of water shoes, ceramics, wine, groceries, and snorkel mask. 

When we attempted to ask the youthful fisherman (fisherkid?) what the peculiar pink flat creatures were in the water, he responded with something like "kathe coochie pi". Google translate interpreted it – certainly incorrectly – as something hilarious and inappropriate, and thus it has become the theme of our trip.
